# Meridian — Go-To-Market Brief

**Audience:** Marketing, Sales, and Partnerships
**Purpose:** Positioning and launch narrative for the Meridian marketplace.

## 1. The Opportunity

Online shoppers are tired of clunky, slow marketplaces that make them re-enter
their details and second-guess whether their order went through. Meridian is the
trustworthy marketplace where buyers shop with confidence and sellers reach new
customers without friction. Our research shows that shoppers abandon carts when
they feel uncertain, and that confidence — not discounts — is the strongest
driver of repeat purchase.

## 2. Positioning

Meridian is positioned as "the marketplace that just works." The brand promise is
speed, trust, and clarity at every step of the shopping journey. We compete on
customer experience and seller success rather than on price alone.

| Pillar | Customer Promise |
| --- | --- |
| Trust | Buyers always know the status of their purchase. |
| Speed | Browsing and buying feel instant. |
| Reach | Sellers find the right buyers quickly. |

## 3. Target Segments

- **Everyday shoppers** seeking a dependable place to buy.
- **Independent sellers** who want a low-friction storefront.
- **Brands** looking for a premium marketplace channel.

## 4. Launch Narrative

The launch campaign, "Shop with confidence," runs across social, search, and
partner channels. We lead with customer testimonials and a 30-day satisfaction
promise. Partnerships with well-known independent brands anchor credibility in
the first quarter.

## 5. Success Metrics (Business)

Marketing measures success by brand awareness lift, new-customer acquisition cost,
repeat-purchase rate, and seller sign-ups. These are go-to-market outcomes owned
by the marketing and sales organizations.
